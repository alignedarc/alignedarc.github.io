A Short Story About Me

I’ve watched too many workshops die.

Not in the room—people are engaged, having real breakthroughs. Someone always says, “Oh… that’s why we keep getting stuck.”

But two weeks later, everyone’s back at their desks.

The same roadmap debates.
The same unclear decisions.
The same planning chaos.

The insight was real—but it didn’t change how the team actually worked.

After years of coaching teams, I realized something critical:

Understanding why a team struggles doesn’t automatically change how the team works.

Knowing strengths doesn’t fix prioritization.
Awareness alone doesn’t reshape execution.

Team dynamics can’t stay abstract.
They have to plug directly into the systems teams run every day—planning, prioritization, and decision-making.

That’s why I created Aligned Arc.

Not just insight—but insight with integration.
