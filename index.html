Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our team workshops, offsites, and facilitation approach.

What types of team workshops does Aligned Arc offer?

Aligned Arc offers three categories of workshops for engineering and product teams in India:

  • Quick Sessions (2-3 hours): Decision Accelerator, Priority Reset, Sprint Retrospective, Team Friction, and Strategy Clarity workshops.
  • Full-Day Workshops (6-8 hours): Quarterly Planning, Product Roadmap, Team Operating System, Team Kickoff, Performance Breakthrough, OKR Alignment, and Innovation Sprint workshops.
  • Goa Offsite Packages (Multi-day): Lite, Balanced, Productive, and Full Offsite packages with facilitated workshops and curated team activities in Goa.
How do I plan a team offsite in Goa?

Aligned Arc handles team offsite planning in Goa end-to-end. Choose from four packages: Lite (one facilitated session + local recommendations), Balanced (two sessions + team activities), Productive (morning sessions + full logistics), or Full Offsite (complete multi-day strategic retreat). We handle venue recommendations, activity curation, and workshop facilitation so you can focus on your team.

What is a quarterly planning workshop?

A quarterly planning workshop is a full-day (6-8 hour) facilitated session where your engineering team structures the upcoming quarter with clear goals, owners, and accountability. Instead of a top-down slide deck, your team co-creates the plan using our ARC System ‚Äî surfacing what's actually stuck, designing execution frameworks together, and applying them on real decisions before the workshop ends.

Who facilitates the workshops at Aligned Arc?

All workshops are facilitated by Sunidhi, Founder of Aligned Arc. She is a Gallup-certified strengths coach with 7+ years of coaching experience and 1000+ clients coached across corporate India. She specializes in engineering and product team dynamics and is based in Goa, India.

What makes Aligned Arc workshops different from other team workshops?

Three things set Aligned Arc apart:

  • Integration, not just insight: We combine team dynamics with strategic work in the same workshop ‚Äî not two separate sessions.
  • Your framework, not our template: Your team designs and builds the decision framework, so they own it and use it independently.
  • Follow-through built in: Every workshop includes 30-day and 90-day check-ins to make sure frameworks stick and evolve.
Do you run innovation and strategy workshops for engineering teams?

Yes. Our Innovation Sprint is a 2-day intensive workshop designed for engineering teams to generate breakthrough ideas using structured design-thinking frameworks. Our Strategy Clarity workshop (2-3 hours) helps teams translate high-level strategy into actionable execution steps. Both workshops use the ARC System to ensure outcomes stick beyond the session.

Can Aligned Arc facilitate workshops anywhere in India, or only in Goa?

Aligned Arc facilitates team workshops across India ‚Äî remotely or on-location at your office. Our signature Goa Offsite Packages are specifically designed for teams that want to combine strategic workshops with a change of environment. Whether you're in Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi, Hyderabad, or Pune, we can bring the workshop to you or host you in Goa.

What is the ideal team size for a workshop?

Our workshops work best with teams of 5-25 people. Quick Sessions (2-3 hours) can be effective for smaller teams of 5-10, while Full-Day Workshops and Goa Offsites work well for larger groups of 10-25. For bigger groups, we design breakout structures to keep engagement high.
