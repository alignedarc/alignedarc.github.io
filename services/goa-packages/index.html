Lite Escape

1 night, 2 days 8–15 people

You need a break from the office. Team needs time away. But can't do 3 days. Lite Escape: arrive Friday afternoon, leave Saturday evening. One core workshop. Rest is decompression.

The Flow

  • Friday afternoon: Arrive in Goa. Check in. Beach walk to shake off the city energy.
  • Friday evening: Dinner at a local spot (not tourist zone). Get to know each other outside work context.
  • Saturday morning: Core workshop (pick one: Quarterly Planning, Team Operating System, Innovation Engine). Full focus. 6 hours.
  • Saturday afternoon: Beach time. Completely unstructured. Decompress.
  • Saturday evening: Leave for airport/home.

What's Included

  • One core workshop (your choice)
  • Venue for workshop (beachside or quiet villa)
  • Experience design (Friday dinner recommendation, Saturday beach spot)
  • Facilitation by me

Not Included

  • Accommodation (team books their own — I can recommend)
  • Travel to/from Goa
  • Meals beyond Friday dinner

Pick This If

  • Can't do multi-day offsite but need a reset
  • Want focused work + team decompression in 36 hours
  • Testing Goa offsites before committing to longer

Balanced Offsite

2 nights, 3 days 8–15 people

Most popular package. Sweet spot between work and reset. Two core workshops. Mornings = focused work. Afternoons = team experiences. Evenings = decompression.

The Flow

  • Thursday evening: Arrive. Casual welcome dinner. No agenda. Just decompress from travel.
  • Friday morning: Workshop 1 (6 hours). Pick from core workshops.
  • Friday afternoon: Team experience (water sports, local market tour, beach cricket — whatever fits team energy).
  • Friday evening: Dinner. Unstructured.
  • Saturday morning: Workshop 2 (6 hours). Different workshop, builds on Friday.
  • Saturday afternoon: Pure beach time. No agenda. Books, swimming, napping.
  • Saturday evening: Closing dinner. Team reflection (optional, casual).
  • Sunday morning: Leave.

What's Included

  • Two core workshops (your choice)
  • Venue for both workshops
  • Friday team experience (designed for your team)
  • Experience design (all meals, locations, flow)
  • Facilitation

Not Included

  • Accommodation
  • Travel
  • Meals (though I design the experience)

Pick This If

  • Want meaningful work + actual team connection
  • Need two complementary workshops (e.g., Quarterly Planning + OKR Alignment)
  • Team needs both: strategic work AND decompression

Most common pairing: Quarterly Planning + Team Operating System

Productive Retreat

2 nights, 3 days 8–15 people

You're not here to relax. You're here to GET WORK DONE away from distractions. Three workshops in 2.5 days. Mornings and evenings = work. Afternoons = quick reset. This is productivity-focused.

The Schedule

  • Thursday evening: Arrive. Quick dinner. Early to bed (tomorrow's intense).
  • Friday 9am–3pm: Workshop 1 (with breaks).
  • Friday 3–5pm: Beach walk or pool time (recharge).
  • Friday 6–9pm: Workshop 2.
  • Saturday 9am–3pm: Workshop 3.
  • Saturday afternoon: Free time.
  • Saturday evening: Closing discussion + dinner.
  • Sunday morning: Leave.

What's Included

  • Three core workshops
  • Venue for all sessions
  • Meals designed for energy/focus (not heavy lunches that make you sleepy)
  • Experience design for maximum productivity

Not Included

  • Accommodation
  • Travel to/from Goa

Pick This If

  • Need to accomplish major strategic work
  • Want multi-workshop momentum (each builds on previous)
  • Team energized by intense focus, not "team building"

Common combo: Quarterly Planning + Product Roadmap + OKR Alignment (full strategic stack)
